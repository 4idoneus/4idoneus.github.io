Welcome
Hello there! I didn’t expect you to stumble upon this page, but here we are. This is my guide on how to be an average Joe in IT — and at the same time, it’s also my personal website, diary, and blog-like to-do list.

This guide concisely covers the essentials of IT from a beginner’s perspective — things I’ve learned, mistakes I’ve made, and practical insights that might help you along the way. Whether you’re just starting out or simply curious, you’ll find simple explanations and examples that make IT a little less intimidating.

I’ll be sharing everything I know about IT in this guide, but I’m still learning, so there might be mistakes or areas where my knowledge is incomplete. If you spot anything that seems off, or if you have questions, suggestions, or just want to share your thoughts, feel free to reach out to me via the contact page.

Think of this space as both a learning resource and a journey log — where I collect experiences, refine my skills, and (hopefully) make technology a bit clearer for others too.
